<pre>Overrides the wire name of one record field or one nullary union case. Without it, field wire
 names follow the generation run's naming policy (camelCase by default) and case tags are the camelCased
 case name.</pre>

## Signature

<div class="fsdocs-usage">
<code>type WireNameAttribute</code>
</div>
